ATMs are Automated Teller Machines that are used to carry day-to-day financial transactions. 
ATMs can be used to withdraw money or to deposit money or even to know the information of an account 
like the balance amount, etc. They are convenient and easy to use,and  it allows consumers to perform 
quick self-service transactions.

In this article, we will discuss the ATM Management System in C++ which is an application that provides 
users with every aspect that an actual Automated Teller Machine i.e., ATM should have.
